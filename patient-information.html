Patient Information

Everything you need to know before your visit to Perfect 32 Dental Care.

First Visit Checklist

Please bring the following to your first appointment:

  • A valid photo ID (Aadhaar, PAN, passport, or driving licence)
  • Previous dental records and X-rays (if available)
  • List of current medications and known allergies
  • Insurance card or policy details (if applicable)
  • Your appointment confirmation SMS/email
  • Arrive 10 minutes early for registration paperwork

Payment Methods

Accepted Payments

  • Cash
  • All major debit & credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay)
  • UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm)
  • Net banking

Insurance

Insurance via prior authorization — please call us at +91 96769 95599 before your visit to confirm your coverage and any pre-approval steps.

Hygiene & Safety Protocols

Your safety is our top priority. Here are the measures we follow:

  • All reusable instruments are autoclave-sterilized between patients
  • Single-use disposables for all contact items (gloves, masks, bibs)
  • HEPA air filtration in all treatment rooms
  • Surface disinfection between every patient appointment
  • Staff PPE including gloves, masks, and face shields
  • Hand sanitization stations at entry and exit

Clinic Accessibility

Facilities

  • Ground floor access
  • Wheelchair accessible
  • Child-friendly waiting area
  • Nearby two & four-wheeler parking

Getting Here

Near Elephant Junction on Pragathi Nagar Road. Easily accessible from KPHB, Kukatpally, Miyapur, and surrounding areas. Landmark: Apurupa Township.
