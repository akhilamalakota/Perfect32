About Perfect 32 Dental Care

Serving families in Pragathi Nagar since 2011 with compassionate, high-quality dental care.

Our Story

Founded in 2011 by Dr. Uday Reddy, Perfect 32 Dental Care was established with a clear mission: to provide Pragathi Nagar and the wider Kukatpally community with world-class dental care in a comfortable, patient-centered environment.

Over the past 13+ years, we've grown from a single-chair practice to a modern multi-specialty dental clinic equipped with the latest technology, including laser dentistry, digital X-rays, and rotary endodontics. Through it all, our commitment to gentle, thorough care has never wavered.

With a 4.8-star rating from over 170 patient reviews, we're proud to be among the most trusted dental clinics in the Pragathi Nagar area. Our team treats every patient like family — from children experiencing their first dental visit to seniors seeking restorative care.

Our Mission

To deliver compassionate, evidence-based dental care using modern technology while ensuring every patient feels safe, informed, and valued. We believe everyone deserves a healthy, confident smile.

Technology & Sterilization

We invest continuously in the latest dental technology to offer safer, faster, and more comfortable treatments:

  • Advanced laser systems for minimally invasive soft-tissue procedures
  • Digital radiography for precise diagnostics with minimal radiation
  • Rotary endodontic instruments for faster, more predictable root canals
  • Autoclave sterilization of all reusable instruments
  • Single-use disposables and HEPA air filtration in operatories

Community Involvement

We regularly participate in free dental check-up camps in local schools and community centres around Pragathi Nagar and Kukatpally. We believe in educating our community about preventive oral care to build healthier smiles from an early age.
