Cosmetic Dentistry

Teeth whitening, veneers, and smile makeovers to restore your confidence with beautiful, natural-looking results.

Who It's For

If you're unhappy with the colour, shape, alignment, or overall appearance of your smile, cosmetic dentistry can help. Whether it's stained teeth, chips, gaps, or uneven gums — we have solutions for every concern.

Our Cosmetic Services

  • Professional Teeth Whitening: In-office whitening for dramatically brighter teeth in a single session
  • Dental Veneers: Thin porcelain/composite shells to correct shape, colour, and minor alignment issues
  • Smile Makeovers: Complete smile redesign combining multiple cosmetic procedures
  • Tooth Bonding: Composite resin to repair chips, gaps, and discoloration
  • Gum Contouring: Reshaping the gum line for a balanced, symmetrical smile

Duration & Recovery

Teeth Whitening: 60–90 minutes, immediate results, no downtime.

Veneers: 2–3 appointments over 2 weeks. Minimal sensitivity for a few days.
