General Dentistry

Comprehensive dental exams to keep oral health in top shape. Safe, pain-minimized extractions and restorative fillings.

Who It's For

General dentistry is for patients of all ages seeking routine dental care — whether it's a regular check-up, cavity treatment, or tooth extraction. If you haven't seen a dentist in a while, a general consultation is the best place to start.

What to Expect

Your visit begins with a comprehensive oral examination using digital diagnostics. We'll assess your teeth, gums, and jaw, and take X-rays if needed. Based on findings, we'll discuss a personalized treatment plan. Common treatments include:

  • Dental examinations and diagnostics
  • Fillings (composite, GIC) for cavities
  • Pain-free tooth extractions under local anaesthesia
  • Crown and bridge work
  • Gum therapy for early gum disease

Duration & Recovery

Duration: 30–60 minutes for a routine check-up; complex procedures may need 1–2 appointments.

Recovery: Most procedures require no downtime. For extractions, we'll provide aftercare instructions — most patients return to normal activities within 24–48 hours.
