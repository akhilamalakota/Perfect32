Paediatric Dentistry

Child-friendly dental care with gentle techniques and a fun, welcoming environment.

Who It's For

Designed for infants, children, and adolescents. Dr. Aarti Uday specializes in making dental visits enjoyable for kids — from their first tooth to their teens.

Our Paediatric Services

  • First dental visit for infants (by age 1)
  • Preventive fluoride treatments and dental sealants
  • Cavity treatment with child-friendly techniques
  • Behavior management for anxious children
  • Space maintainers for premature tooth loss
  • Habit counseling (thumb-sucking, tongue-thrusting)
  • Emergency dental care for injured teeth

Child-Friendly Environment

Our clinic features colourful waiting area seating, a warm atmosphere, and staff trained in paediatric behavior management. We use the "tell-show-do" technique to help children feel comfortable and engaged.
