Laser Dentistry

Minimally invasive soft-tissue procedures with faster healing and greater precision.

Who It's For

Ideal for patients seeking minimally invasive alternatives, especially those with dental anxiety. Many laser procedures need less or no anaesthesia.

Procedures We Offer

  • Gum Contouring — Precise reshaping for a balanced smile
  • Frenectomy — Tongue-tie/lip-tie removal with minimal bleeding
  • Periodontal Treatment — Laser-assisted deep cleaning
  • Lesion Removal — Oral soft-tissue biopsy and removal
  • Whitening Enhancement — Laser-activated for faster results

Advantages

✓ Less pain · ✓ Faster healing · ✓ Reduced bleeding · ✓ Greater precision · ✓ Preserves healthy tissue

Duration & Recovery

Duration: 15–60 min. Recovery: Most patients resume normal activities same day.
