Root Canal Treatment

Save infected teeth with modern rotary endodontics and advanced pain control for a comfortable, effective treatment.

Who It's For

Root canal treatment (endodontics) is recommended when the soft tissue inside your tooth (the pulp) becomes infected or inflamed. Common signs include persistent toothache, sensitivity to hot/cold, swollen gums, or darkening of the tooth.

What to Expect

At Perfect 32, we use modern rotary endodontic instruments and advanced imaging to perform root canals efficiently and comfortably:

  • Digital X-ray to assess the extent of infection
  • Local anaesthesia for a completely painless experience
  • Infected pulp removal using NiTi rotary files
  • Thorough cleaning and shaping of root canals
  • Sealing with biocompatible material
  • Crown placement for long-term protection (recommended)

Duration & Recovery

Duration: Typically 1–2 sessions of 45–90 minutes each, depending on complexity.

Recovery: Mild discomfort for 2–3 days, manageable with over-the-counter pain relievers. You can eat normally after the numbness wears off. Avoid chewing on the treated side until the permanent crown is placed.
